About Me

Who I Am

I am Emmanuel Kiprotich Kirui, an experienced ICT Officer & Web Developer based in Nairobi, Kenya. With a solid foundation in both hardware and software, I bridge the gap between infrastructure management and modern web development — helping organisations across Kenya harness the full power of digital technology.

My career spans IT helpdesk support, server administration, cloud architecture on Google Cloud Platform, and building custom web applications using PHP, Python, and JavaScript. I hold a Diploma in Information Technology from Kisii University and have supplemented my education with industry certifications including the Google Cloud Cybersecurity Certificate and several other credentials.

I am particularly passionate about cloud computing and its potential to leapfrog traditional infrastructure challenges in the East African context.

Outside of work, I mentor upcoming ICT students, contribute to open-source projects on GitHub, and write about practical technology adoption in Africa. I am actively seeking opportunities in IT administration, cloud engineering, systems support, or full-stack web development.
